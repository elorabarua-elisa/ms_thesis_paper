\chapter{Conclusion}

This paper has provided an overview of prequalification tests for two popular ancillar services: \textit{FCR} and \textit{aFRR}. The prequalification test provide permission to the interested participants to bid in the market and serve ancillay services and it is a crucial step for the market participants to demonstrate the strength of their system to take actions in the power system. The electricity market always follow a strandard structure for all market actors but a inevitable differences are introduced in balancing market within short time. Thus, some changes are expected in the prequalification test for FCR and aFRR markets all time.
