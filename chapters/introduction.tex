\subsection{Background and Motivation}
Stablish a balanced supply-demand chain in electricity system and precautionary steps to prevent grid blackout or system damage is essential for the welfare of a country. A qualified authority can control national grid with proper actions on time.  

Reserve Market:  
Reserves are necessary to deal with uncertainty inherent to electricity systems, such as forced outages and forecast errors. They are used to maintain the supply-demand balance at all times. This type of reserves can be provided by power plants, renewable generation units, storage units or flexible load. System operators procure reserves (well) before the moment of delivery and activate them in real-time when needed to deal with imbalances.
